FAQs

How to Post Styles to the Feed:

It’s very important to preset opening hours, team member list if any, and location of your shop/ store on trendit 1- Tap Schedule from Menu 2- From Schedule screen tap on the 3 dots option on the top right corner 3- Select the corresponding option and do the following: • Setup Opening Hours: The flexible dialogue box contains opening and closing hours enable providers to have total control over their work hours from opening through break to closing hour • Add Team Members: Team Member dialogue box allows you to add and remove a team member at any time. The app manages each member schedule based on their skills • Add Contact Information: Adding your shop/store location on trendit will help client to easily find you

How can a Service Provider add a non-trendit user to appointment?

Adding styles’ name and price help clients to easily shop with you. 1- Tap Schedule from Menu 2- On calendar screen, tap on the plus (+) icon next to the service category 3- Fill out the style information form (Style name, Price, Who does this, and duration) 4- Tap add button at the bottom of the form and Save

How can a Service Provider Setup Their Schedule on trendit?

It’s very important to preset opening hours, team member list if any, and location of your shop/ store on trendit 1- Tap Schedule from Menu 2- From Schedule screen tap on the 3 dots option on the top right corner 3- Select the corresponding option and do the following: • Setup Opening Hours: The flexible dialogue box contains opening and closing hours enable providers to have total control over their work hours from opening through break to closing hour • Add Team Members: Team Member dialogue box allows you to add and remove a team member at any time. The app manages each member schedule based on their skills • Add Contact Information: Adding your shop/store location on trendit will help client to easily find you

How to Add Styles names and prices on trendit:

How to Add Styles names and prices on trendit: Adding styles’ name and price help clients to easily shop with you. 1- Tap Schedule from Menu 2- On calendar screen, tap on the plus (+) icon next to the service category 3- Fill out the style information form (Style name, Price, Who does this, and duration) 4- Tap add button at the bottom of the form and Save Notice here that you have the option to set price range for a style and also the option to assign a team member to a style Tap the Add button to add the style to your category and repeat if necessary and Save to keep all the styles to your schedule.

Edit Category:

All the styles in each category of service providers’ schedule can always be edited. Here’s how 1. Tap Schedule from menu 2. On calendar screen, tap the pen icon 3. Select the style you want to edit to display the style information 4. Tap on update button to save the changes made on that style

Update Calendar:

trendit makes it easier for service providers to manage their schedule and team members effectively. With trendit calendar you may plan for a day, a week, a month or an entire year. To do this; 1. Tap Schedule from menu 2. Select day, week or month from calendar 3. Tap update button 4. Toggle to turn off the selected date if you want to turn off the selected dates or; 5. Select from team members list to mark availability 6. Tap on Save button to validate your update Note that any changes you make here on the Update Date(s) page will affect all the selected dates
